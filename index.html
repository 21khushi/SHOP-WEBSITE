Sikka Trading Co. - About Us

Sikka Trading Co. is a renowned name in the market specializing in the trade of high-quality disposable items. With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, we have established ourselves as a trusted supplier for a wide range of disposable products, ensuring convenience, hygiene, and sustainability for our customers.
